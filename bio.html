Biography

Samantha Orr started playing the violin from the age of 4 and quickly discovered she was a born musician. She quickly climbed the ranks until she obtained her RCM certification. Cocurrently she also found her passion for piano and also quickly mastered it.

Samantha soon found she couldnt get enough of music, she joined the Kitchener Waterloo Youth Orchastra as well as the Georgian Bay Symphony.

Soon after she started competing in local Kiwinis Festivals and astounded the judges as she won time and time again in multiple catagorys in both piano and violin advancing to provincals more then once.

However music isn't her only passion. Following in her fathers (Dr.Richard Orr) she found another passion in the sciences. She love for biology was apparent was aparent right away and it only grew over the years and Samantha sped through highschool taking advanced courses at a French Immersion highschool as well as a regular one. She was a very gifted bright and achomplished student earn praise and admiration from her peers and teachers. She also made a spot apperance in a local newspaper for her top placement in the University of Toronto Nation Biology Exam (link to article). She gradutated with honours as well as a SHSM and is continuing her post secondary at Western University studying Medical Sciences with a combined degree with Music with the hopes to get into med school.
